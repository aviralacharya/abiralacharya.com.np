I started my SEO journey out of curiosity — wondering why some websites appear at the top of Google while others stay buried. That curiosity turned into a passion, and that passion turned into a career.

Based in Birtamode, Jhapa, I've worked with businesses across Nepal — from small local shops in the eastern Terai to e-commerce brands in Kathmandu — helping them build a sustainable organic presence on Google.

My approach is simple: understand the business, understand the audience, then build an SEO strategy that connects the two. No shortcuts. No black-hat tricks. Just methodical, research-backed SEO that compounds over time.
