When Darumandu came to me in January 2026, the site had near-zero organic presence. It was an e-commerce platform built for a genuinely underserved need — 24-hour online liquor delivery in Kathmandu — but Google had no idea it existed. There was no schema, a broken robots.txt, redirect chains, no internal linking strategy, and content that didn’t match how people actually searched.

Four months later: 9,020+ total organic clicks, 340,000+ impressions, a 229% year-over-year click increase, and the brand appearing as the #1 recommendation in Google’s AI Mode and ChatGPT when anyone searches for late-night alcohol delivery in Kathmandu. This is the full breakdown of exactly what was done.

ℹ️

Project scope: Technical SEO, on-page content, schema markup, Core Web Vitals, internal linking, and AI/LLM brand visibility strategy. Site: darumandu.com — Nepal’s 24-hour online liquor delivery platform. Engagement start: January 2026.

Project Background — What is Darumandu?

Darumandu is Nepal’s trusted 24-hour online liquor delivery service, operating across the Kathmandu Valley with domestic and imported spirits — whisky, beer, wine, vodka, and rum. The business runs around the clock with a transparent late-night service fee, positioning itself as the most reliable option for consumers needing alcohol delivered at any hour.

The Nepali market for online liquor delivery is nascent but growing fast. Search demand spikes around late-night hours, festivals, and social occasions. The core SEO opportunity: capture this demand before competitors could establish domain authority.

Primary objectives set at the start of the engagement:

  • Build topical authority around 24-hour liquor delivery in Kathmandu
  • Dominate local and transactional search intent for the Nepali market
  • Establish brand presence in Google AI Mode, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity
  • Achieve 2,000+ organic clicks per 28-day window within Q1 2026
  • Pass Core Web Vitals on all key landing pages

Keyword Clustering & Search Intent Mapping

The foundation of the entire strategy was a cluster-based keyword architecture — not just a flat list of keywords, but a structured grouping by search intent that maps every keyword to a specific page type and content goal. Three intent layers were identified:

🛒 Transactional (Primary)
  • late night alcohol delivery kathmandu
  • 24 hours liquor delivery kathmandu
  • online liquor delivery nepal
  • spirits delivery kathmandu
  • whisky delivery kathmandu night
  • order alcohol online nepal
  • beer delivery kathmandu anytime
📖 Informational (Supporting)
  • how to order alcohol online nepal
  • best liquor brands in kathmandu
  • imported whisky nepal price
  • liquor delivery service nepal review
  • 24 hour shop kathmandu
🏷️ Navigational / Brand
  • darumandu delivery
  • darumandu liquor nepal
  • darumandu kathmandu service fee

Each cluster was mapped to a specific URL group — ensuring topical depth and zero keyword cannibalization. Transactional pages were built purely for conversion. Informational pages were built for trust-building and AI citability.

💡

Why clustering matters for Nepal SEO: Most Nepal e-commerce sites target a long list of keywords on one page, confusing Google about the page’s purpose. A cluster model tells Google: this is the authoritative page for this specific intent — faster ranking, less internal competition, stronger topical authority signals.

Technical SEO Fixes — The Foundation

Before any content work began, the technical foundation was overhauled. A Screaming Frog crawl on day one revealed critical issues — all resolved in the first two weeks.

robots.txt Repair

The original robots.txt was misconfigured, blocking Googlebot from crawling key product and category pages. A corrected file was implemented allowing full crawl coverage while excluding only admin and checkout paths. Without this fix, none of the subsequent content work would have been indexed.

Sitemap Restructuring

The XML sitemap was rebuilt to reflect the new URL architecture, prioritising category pages (spirits, beer, wine) and high-intent landing pages. Lastmod timestamps were added and the sitemap submitted via GSC to trigger immediate re-crawl.

URL Structure Optimisation

Legacy URLs were generic and keyword-free. Every primary URL was restructured to a keyword-inclusive, human-readable format:

  • /24-hour-liquor-delivery-kathmandu/
  • /whisky-delivery-nepal/
  • /beer-order-online-kathmandu/

All legacy URLs were 301-redirected. Redirect chains (A→B→C hops) were collapsed to direct 301s (A→C) to preserve full link equity.

Core Web Vitals — Homepage Cluster

The homepage was optimised to pass Google’s Core Web Vitals thresholds. Key improvements: LCP via image compression and lazy loading; CLS by reserving layout space for above-the-fold images; INP via script deferral and main-thread unblocking. All three moved from “Needs Improvement” to “Good” within three weeks.

On-Page SEO & Semantic Content Strategy

All primary landing pages were rewritten with a semantic content approach — each page targeting a primary keyword cluster while incorporating semantically related terms naturally in the copy, allowing Google to understand topical relevance without keyword stuffing.

Homepage as Topical Hub

The homepage was restructured to function as the cluster anchor for the entire site. A semantic content block covered: what Darumandu offers, which Kathmandu Valley areas it serves, alcohol types available, delivery hours, pricing model, and a short trust-building “About Us” section in the footer.

Short “About Us” Footer Section

A concise About section was added to the footer of every page — anchoring Darumandu as Nepal’s most trusted 24-hour alcohol delivery service. This establishes E-E-A-T signals across the entire site and gives LLMs a citable, consistent entity description to reference.

Search Intent Alignment

Product category pages (whisky, vodka, rum, beer, wine) served both informational and transactional intent: brand descriptions alongside clear order CTAs. Every page either converted or built trust that contributed to conversion.

Structured Data & Schema Markup

Comprehensive schema markup was the single biggest driver of Darumandu’s AI Mode and LLM visibility. Structured data makes content machine-readable — exactly what Google’s AI Overview extraction algorithm and LLM training pipelines are optimised to process.

LocalBusiness Schema

A full LocalBusiness schema was added to the homepage including businessName, address (Kathmandu), openingHours (24/7), telephone, priceRange, and areaServed (Kathmandu Valley). This directly contributed to Darumandu’s appearance in Google Maps panels and AI-generated local summaries.

Product Schema

Every product page received Product schema with offers (price in NPR, availability: InStock) and aggregateRating. This improved product visibility in Google Shopping and rich results — and the 5.0 star rating displayed in ChatGPT came directly from this structured data.

BreadcrumbList Schema

BreadcrumbList schema was implemented sitewide, enabling breadcrumb SERP features and reducing duplicate content signals by clearly establishing the canonical page at each hierarchy level.

Internal Linking Architecture

A systematic internal linking structure was built to pass authority from high-traffic pages down to cluster target pages:

  • Homepage → all primary cluster pages using exact-match and partial-match keyword anchor text
  • Every category page → relevant product pages using descriptive contextual anchors
  • Informational content → transactional pages using keyword-rich anchors aligned to the target cluster
  • No generic anchors — “click here” and “read more” replaced with descriptive keyword-aligned terms throughout

🤖 Why Darumandu Gets Cited by AI — The Technical Reasons

LLMs and Google’s AI systems cite brands that meet a specific set of signals. Here is exactly what drove Darumandu’s AI visibility:

  • LocalBusiness schema with consistent NAP — established Darumandu as a verified, machine-readable local entity with clear geographic association
  • Semantic FAQ-style content — key pages included direct Q&A sections matching the question-answer patterns LLMs extract
  • High GSC click volume + strong position — 9k+ clicks and avg position 7.5 signal high relevance to AI training pipelines
  • Brand mentions across trusted sources — local directory listings, Ghyampay guides, and third-party reviews reinforced entity authority
  • Concise About Us footer copy — gave LLMs a clean, citable entity summary: who Darumandu is, what they do, where they operate
  • Product schema with aggregateRating — the 5.0 star rating in ChatGPT came directly from structured data on the site
