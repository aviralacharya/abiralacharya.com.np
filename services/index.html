❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How long does SEO take to show results in Nepal?
Typically 3–6 months for meaningful ranking improvements, though technical fixes and local SEO can show results faster — often within 4–8 weeks. SEO is a long-term investment; the compounding returns over 12+ months far outweigh any short-term channel.
Do you work with businesses outside Jhapa / Eastern Nepal?
Absolutely. I work with businesses across all of Nepal — Kathmandu, Pokhara, Biratnagar, Chitwan, and beyond. All work is done remotely with regular video/WhatsApp check-ins, so location is no barrier at all.
What's the difference between On-Page and Technical SEO?
On-Page SEO focuses on the content and HTML of individual pages (titles, headings, content relevance, internal links). Technical SEO focuses on how the site is built and how Google crawls it (site speed, crawl errors, structured data, indexing). Both are essential — one without the other leaves rankings on the table.
Do you guarantee rankings?
No honest SEO consultant guarantees specific rankings — Google's algorithm is their property, not ours. What I guarantee is a rigorous, white-hat process, full transparency, and measurable progress toward your organic traffic goals. Anyone promising "#1 on Google in 30 days" should be avoided.
What information do you need to get started?
To start, I need access to your Google Search Console and Google Analytics (read-only is fine), your website URL, and a brief on your business goals. Everything else I'll gather through the audit and discovery process.
Can you help with Nepali-language SEO content?
Yes. I conduct keyword research in both English and Nepali and can advise on bilingual content strategy. For Nepali-language content writing, I work with trusted Nepali copywriters to ensure linguistic quality.
